locale 有时会有问题，编辑 /etc/default/locale： 

LANG=C.UTF-8
LANGUAGE=C.UTF-8
LC_ALL=C.UTF-8

https://mirrors.163.com/.help/debian.html

可以粗暴点： 

echo 'deb http://mirrors.163.com/debian/ buster main non-free contrib' >/etc/apt/sources.list
